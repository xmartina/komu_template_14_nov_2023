ADMISSION FOR 2021/2022 SESSION

 

ADMISSION! ADMISSION! ADMISSION! 

OFFER OF SUPPLEMENTARY ADMISSION SPACES

 

Kingsley Ozumba Mbadiwe University offers supplementary admission into its regular undergraduate programmes to qualified candidates (with score of 120 and above in UTME) in the ongoing 2021/2022 admission exercise

 

AVAILABLE COURSES:

Faculty of Sciences:

ѵ  Biochemistry (B.Sc.)                     ѵ  Microbiology (B.Sc)

ѵ  Zoology (B.Sc)                              ѵ  Computer Science (B.Sc)

ѵ  Botany (B.Sc)                                ѵ  Physics (B.Sc)

ѵ  Chemistry (B.Sc)                           ѵ  Biology (B.Sc)

ѵ  Mathematics (B.Sc)                       ѵ  Statistics (B.Sc)

 

Faculty of Management Sciences:

ѵ  Accounting (B.Sc)                        ѵ  Business Administration (B.Sc)

ѵ  Banking & Finance (B.Sc)            ѵ  Economics (B.Sc)

ѵ  Political Science (B.Sc)                 ѵ  Sociology (B.Sc)

ѵ  Psychology (B.Sc)                         ѵ  Management (B.Sc)

Faculty of Arts:

ѵ  History & International Studies (B.A)

ѵ  Linguistics (B.A)

Faculty of Environmental Sciences:

ѵ  Architecture (B.Sc)

ѵ  Estate Management (B.Sc)

 

HOW TO APPLY

 

Interested candidates should strictly follow the under-listed steps to be considered for admission.

Ø Change your University of first choice to Kingsley Ozumba Mbadiwe University on JAMB portal

Ø Upload your O’level results in JAMB CAPS.

Ø Visit the KOMU website at www.komu.edu.ng; Click on Start the application process; fill out the form completely and Submit.

 

Screening of already admitted candidates holds at the University Campus at Ogboko on the following days:

Ø Thursday, 3rd February 2022

Ø Friday 4th February 2022

Ø Tuesday 8th February 2022 (Last Day)

 

Hurry while the offer lasts!!

 

 

For further inquiries please call the Admissions Officer on 08034646192 or visit the KOMU website at www.komu.edu.ng for details.
