KOMU recognized & listed on JAMB CAPS

KOMU recognized & listed on JAMB CAPS

November 02, 2021

Ikechukwu Nosike DOZIE

The Vice-Chancellor of Kingsley Ozumba Mbadiwe University KOMU, Professor Ikechukwu N.S Dozie yesterday visited the SA to Governor Hope Uzodimma, Abuja Liason, Dr Jimmy Imo while on fact finding mission to JAMB.

 

At JAMB Headquarters Abuja, the KOMU Chief Executive ensured that KOMU was listed and captured on JAMB CAPS for ease of online admissions.

 

Having emberked on such visit severally in the past, the November first visit proved confirmatory and KOMU is now active on JAMB portal. 

 

The Vice-Chancellor was accompanied on the visit by his Executive Assistant & Coordinator Vice-Chancellor's Office, Barr. Obinna Igwe.

 

Hippo A Nwachukwu.

Ag PRO KOMU.

2nd Nov. 2021.
