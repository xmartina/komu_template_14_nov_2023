2023/2024 Regular and JUPEB Admissions are Ongoing !!

Click here to fill out your undergraduate admissions application form

Click here to fill out your JUPEB admissions application form

KOMU invites candidates with UTME scores of 140 and above, who desire admission into its NUC-approved undergraduate academic programmes for the 2023/2024 academic session.

Candidates who did not choose KOMU as their first choice institution can apply for our regular admission by effecting a change of institution on the JAMB Portal.

Candidates who scored below or above 140 can apply for our JUPEB Program
